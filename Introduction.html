Introduction

What are typhoons, and what is Typhoon Parma?

Typhoons are defined as a tropical cyclone with a maximum wind of 34 knots or higher. A tropical cyclone with the maximum wind of less than 34 knots is called a tropical depression. The difference between two types of tropical cyclones are just if the maximum wind is less than or above the threshold of 34 knots.

Typhoon Pepeng or Typhoon Parma formed from a tropical depression east of the Philippines on October 2, 2009. The tropical depression was upgraded to a tropical named “Pepeng” by PAGASA, Pepeng was upgraded to a typhoon on October 9, 2009 as it moved towards the Philippines. About the geographical coordinates for the path of typhoon pepeng the formation coordinates is around 15°N, 135°E and the location is in the East of the philippines, and to its first landfall in the philippines the coordinates is 18.0°N, 122.0°E and the location is in the cagayan valley region, northern philippines, the movement over northern philippines the coordinates gradually moving westward, staying around 18°N, 121-122°E, the second landfall in the philippines the coordinates is 16.5°N, 120.0°E the location is in the ilocos region, northern philippines, and for the exit from the philippines area of responsibility the coordinates is around 15°N, 155°E and the location is exited the philippine area headed towards China. At the first landfall the maximum Sustained winds is 215 km/h (135 mph) and gusts up to 250 km/h (155 mph), during the movement over northern luzon the maximum sustained winds is 185-195 km/h (115-120 mph) and gusts up to 225-235 km/h (140-145 mph), at the second landfall the maximum sustained winds is 175 km/h (110 mph) and gusts up to 210 km/h (130 mph), and as it exited the philippines the maximum sustained winds is 150-160 km/h (95-100 mph). The visual illustration development of the typhoon is the Satellite Imagery of typhoon formation: This depicts a tropical depression forming east of the Philippines, with spiral cloud bands, which strengthens as the system develops, and the eye and eyewall become more defined.
